About 5036

Our rookie year was 2014 and since then our team has competed in numerous competitions and had amazing results. We had the ability to go to the FIRST Worlds Championship 3 times in the last 6 years. Due to the fact that our team is a close community, many 5036 alumnis have comeback to mentor the new coming members of our team. Since the beginning of our team, we have had many achievements and awards. In 2017, we ranked fourth at two local “District Events" and 7th at the Ontario Provincial Championship. Our performance that season allowed us to qualify for the World Championships in St. Louis. In 2018, we qualified for the World Championships yet again and due to our teams culture we intend to keep improving. This year in 2019, our team won the Creativity Award at Ryerson University for our impressive use of sensors and qualified for the Ontario Provincial Championship.

Building a robot is not our only goal but spreading the knowledge of STEM and programming throughout the members of our team is important because as the world develops STEM education will be vital! In our community, we spend our time showcasing our robot to introducing STEM to younger generations. Team 5036 believes that any goal is possible and we work hard to “Make it Happen”.
